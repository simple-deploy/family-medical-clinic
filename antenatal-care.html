Personalised Pregnancy Care

At MJD Health Medical Clinics, we understand every pregnancy journey is unique. Our antenatal care services are designed to provide comprehensive support, guidance, and reassurance throughout your pregnancy.

From your first prenatal appointment to postnatal guidance, our caring medical professionals work closely with you to monitor your health, support your baby’s development, and answer any questions you may have along the way.

🩺

Routine Checkups

Regular assessments to monitor maternal and baby health throughout pregnancy.

👶

Baby Monitoring

Track your baby's growth and development with professional care.

💚

Health Guidance

Nutrition, wellbeing, lifestyle, and pregnancy support from experienced practitioners.

Our Antenatal Services Include

  • ✔ Pregnancy confirmation and initial assessments
  • ✔ Routine antenatal consultations
  • ✔ Blood pressure and health monitoring
  • ✔ Pregnancy pathology referrals
  • ✔ Ultrasound referrals and guidance
  • ✔ Gestational diabetes screening
  • ✔ Nutrition and lifestyle advice
  • ✔ Mental wellbeing support during pregnancy
  • ✔ Postnatal care and recovery support
