🧠 Caring Mental Health Support

Mental Health

MJD Health Medical Clinics proudly supports patients with compassionate mental health care, counselling referrals, and personalised treatment pathways.

Book Online About Us

MJD Health Medical Clinics are proud to promote experienced GP’s at our Maudsland Medical Centre and Emerald Lakes Medical Clinic who are trained to support patients with mental health concerns.

We strongly encourage anyone concerned about their mental wellbeing to take the first step toward seeking help and making an appointment to discuss their concerns.

Our expert team includes qualified cognitive behavioural therapists who specialise in counselling and hypnosis, helping patients begin their recovery journey.

You are not alone. Each year, many adults across the Gold Coast seek help from a GP for mental illness, anxiety, depression, stress, and emotional wellbeing concerns.

Our GP’s can discuss your concerns, assess your situation, and provide guidance on appropriate next steps and available treatment pathways.

After Your Consultation, Your GP May:

  • ✔ Diagnose physical conditions contributing to symptoms
  • ✔ Refer you to community-based mental health services
  • ✔ Refer you to mental health specialists for assessment or treatment
  • ✔ Continue providing ongoing GP support and treatment
  • ✔ Arrange bulk-billed mental health treatment plans where appropriate

Supporting Gold Coast Communities

MJD Health Medical Clinics proudly support patients throughout Maudsland, Upper Coomera, Pacific Pines, Helensvale, Hope Island, Logan, Nerang, and surrounding Gold Coast communities.
