About Us

MJD Health Family Health owns and operates this premier clinic. We are a mixed-billing Medical Centre in the lovely rural surrounds of the Gold Coast.

Our highly qualified General Practitioners provide friendly, thorough and advanced medical care to all of our patients, with years of experience in a broad range of health problems affecting all age groups. We service our local Gold Coast communities spanning from Upper Coomera to Tweed Heads.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?

In most cases, yes. Our primary care family physicians will conduct an initial evaluation and automatically refer you internally to our specialized wings (such as pediatrics or women's health) as needed.

Are telemedicine consultations available for all services?

We offer telehealth for routine follow-ups, medication refills, and minor urgent care issues (e.g. skin rashes, flu symptoms). Annual physicals and initial diagnostic workups require in-person visits.
