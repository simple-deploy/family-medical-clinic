Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring anything to my immunisation appointment? −

Please bring your Medicare card and any vaccination history or medical records relevant to your appointment.

Are vaccinations painful? +

Most vaccinations only cause mild discomfort for a short period. Our staff aim to make the process as comfortable as possible.

Can I get multiple vaccines at once? +

Yes, depending on medical suitability and vaccine schedules, multiple vaccines may be safely administered during one visit.

How long does the appointment take? +

Most vaccination appointments take approximately 15–30 minutes including assessment and aftercare advice.
