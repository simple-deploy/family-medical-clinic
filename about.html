Our Story

MJD Health Medical Clinics owns and operates this premier clinic. We are a mixed-billing Medical Centre dedicated to bringing world-class healthcare to the lovely rural surrounds of the Gold Coast.

Our highly qualified General Practitioners provide friendly, thorough and advanced medical care to all of our patients, with years of experience in a broad range of health problems affecting all age groups. We service our local Gold Coast communities spanning from Upper Coomera to Tweed Heads.

MJD Health Medical Clinic Service

Our clinic team are experienced, committed, honest and friendly people in the business of providing a comprehensive and up to date health service to you and your family.

Our promise is to engage, educate and assist you in maintaining your health and wellbeing any way that we can. We want our patients to benefit from our high levels of quality services and we will always provide you with that little bit extra to ensure your satisfaction.

We also know that superior service extends beyond just providing a friendly experience for everyone. We have access to a wide distribution network and it is our commitment to get you what you need. And as far as your health goes if we don't know the answer, we will get it for you.

At MJD Health Medical Clinics you can be assured that if it matters to you, it matters to us too!

With up-to-date medical knowledge, techniques, and commitment to the highest standards of care, MJD Health prides ourselves on Quality Health Care in a friendly atmosphere.

Our Mission

The mission we have at our Clinic, is:

  • to ensure a professional, effective service to our patients at all times
  • to ensure an atmosphere where our patients feel comfortable and safe
  • to ensure our patients are confident that their welfare is our priority
  • to maintain a high-quality standard of care
  • to ensure all information relating to our patients is kept securely
